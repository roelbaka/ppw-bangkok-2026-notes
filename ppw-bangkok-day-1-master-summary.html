Cross-cutting themes (merged)

1 · Portals, AI & moats

One line: Winners combine brand + structured data + behaviour/workflow; generic AI search is not enough; LLMs won’t prioritise property first.

Where to go deeper: Turpin · Myers · Parker (schema / agents)

2 · AI: outcomes & discipline, not theatre

One line: Access to AI is table stakes; value = execution, workflow change, measured revenue/cost — not “everyone has ChatGPT.”

Where to go deeper: Kenner (second mover, move the needle) · Anadirekkul · Parker (filters rebranded as AI) · Wee (P&L first)

3 · Listings / ads vs transactions & trust

One line: Scale and margin in emerging markets often favour transaction-led models and execution depth over portal-only; high-ticket lifestyle still needs humans at the close.

Where to go deeper: Kenner · Coleman · Wee

4 · Before the listing: seller intent & prep

One line: Major spend and decisions happen months before a listing hits a portal; platforms that own that phase own a new data and revenue layer.

Where to go deeper: Bugeja (digital twin ecosystem) · Kenner (portals blind to prep phase)

5 · Agents: adviser, not commodity

One line: Tools that surface strategy vs charisma push agents toward trusted adviser positioning; fee becomes secondary if value is clear.

Where to go deeper: Bugeja (structured proposals) · Khan (TruBroker gamification) · Kenner (low-tech broker Q&A)

6 · Data layer: comms, agents, intelligence

One line: Portals and PropTech need call/lead data, agent mapping, and reproducible analytics — not only raw listings.

Where to go deeper: Gallagher · Rose

7 · Monetisation beyond classifieds

One line: Retail media and richer listing UX (e.g. furnished visuals) can turn media from cost centre to revenue and speed decisions.

Where to go deeper: Gray · Anadirekkul (staging / Bangkok Asset)

8 · Global platform & cross-border scale

One line: Full-stack global real estate platform — listings, agents, fulfilment, tech stack, China/regional nuances.

Where to go deeper: Ho

9 · Capital, emerging markets & strategy

One line: VC/investor lens on AI-era marketplaces; FDV-style portfolio view on emerging-market portals.

Where to go deeper: Murakami · Grove · Kenner (M&A / cap tables)

10 · Product-led trust (verification & valuation)

One line: Official data partnerships, verified availability, gamified broker quality, and AI-assisted valuation build trust loops.

Where to go deeper: Khan (TruEstimate, TruCheck, TruBroker, TrueGP) — same product philosophy as several PPW themes but from a different session date.
