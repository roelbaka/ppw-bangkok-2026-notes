Market leadership and product strategy

Ref: 00:02:23

Haider highlighted the company’s leading position in the market, attributing success to a product-centric approach that began in 2014 from a disadvantaged position. The company focused on building value for all stakeholders — consumers, brokers, and the industry — to overcome a significant trust deficit and stagnation in innovation.

Data transparency and property valuation — TruEstimate

Ref: 00:03:39 · 00:05:32

The lack of readily available data was addressed by partnering with the land department to obtain and clean transaction data, which led to TruEstimate. This tool provides comprehensive property valuations and insights, helping bridge the trust gap between consumers and brokers by offering an independent analysis of property value. High adoption of TruEstimate and other tools by agents and homeowners has enabled organic lead generation.

Property availability and broker credibility — TruCheck

Ref: 00:07:44 · 00:08:58

TruCheck was launched in 2019 to verify property availability through a rigorous process involving geo-tagged app snapshots. The system encourages agents to submit verified listings by offering increased visibility. It has significantly improved consumer trust and broker impressions, establishing TruCheck as a brand for reliable properties.

Gamification and social selling — TruBroker

Ref: 00:08:58 · 00:11:08

The TruBroker program is a gamification platform that rewards agents with TruPoints for quality listings, responsiveness, and transaction claims, leading to increased visibility and leads. Coupled with extensive outdoor marketing, it fosters direct engagement between users and top-ranked brokers. TruBroker Stories was launched to enable social selling through video content directly on the platform.

Peer-to-peer networking and immersive floor plans

Ref: 00:14:18 · 00:15:11

A peer-to-peer network for brokers allows them to post client requirements and receive notifications for matching properties, facilitating communication even within the same agency. Significant investment was made in 3D immersive floor plans and the TruScan tool, which enables agents to generate precise 3D dimensions and correlate property images, empowering brokers with better tools for property understanding and sales.

AI for market insights and broker performance

Ref: 00:16:10 · 00:17:24

The company has a long-standing investment in AI. A natural language processing tool, TrueGP, was launched to help users understand property yields and capital appreciation. Plans include AI-powered listing creation and detailed AI-based lead analysis that provides sentiment analysis and feedback to brokers, aiming to improve service quality and foster trust.
